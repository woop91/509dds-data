# Department of Family and Medical Leave   (DFML)

Log In to...


- [Log in or create an account](https://paidleave.mass.gov/)

The Department of Family and Medical Leave oversees the Commonwealth’s Paid Family and Medical Leave (PFML) program. This program provides temporary income replacement to eligible workers who are welcoming a new child into their family, are struck by a serious illness or injury, need to take care of an ill or ailing relative, and for certain military considerations.

## IRS issues extension of PFML tax ruling

**Please be advised that DFML will delay its implementation of certain portions of the tax withholding and reporting requirements outlined in IRS Revenue Ruling 2025-4 due to new guidance issued by the IRS.** On December 19, 2025, the Internal Revenue Service (IRS) issued IRS Notice 2026-6 which extends the implementation transition period provided to state paid family and medical leave programs and employers for an additional year.

[Learn more about how this impacts employers in Massachusetts](https://www.mass.gov/info-details/paid-family-and-medical-leave-pfml-tax-information-for-employers)

# Illinois (IL) DDS Salary Research

**Last Updated:** 2026-06-02 (AUTHORITATIVE — IL Administrative Code RC-062 table + CMS AFSCME RC-062 rates)

## Administering Agency
**Illinois Department of Human Services (IDHS), Bureau of Disability Determination Services** — SSA cooperative agreement.

## Examiner Classification (VERIFIED — IL Administrative Code Table W, RC-062)
IL's actual class is the **Disability Claims Adjudicator** series (not "Disability Adjudicator"). Class title -> RC-062 pay grade verified from the official Illinois Administrative Code (Title 80, Part 310, Appendix A, Table W — RC-062 Technical unit):

| Class Title | Title Code | Bargaining Unit | Pay Grade |
|---|---|---|---|
| Disability Claims Adjudicator Trainee | 12539 | RC-062 | 13 |
| Disability Claims Adjudicator I | 12537 | RC-062 | 16 |
| Disability Claims Adjudicator II | 12538 | RC-062 | 18 |
| Disability Claims Specialist | 12558 | RC-062 | 19 |
| Disability Claims Analyst | 12540 | RC-062 | 21 |
| Disability Appeals Officer | 12530 | RC-062 | 22 |

The core examiner ladder is Trainee → Adjudicator I → Adjudicator II; the senior/lead in-series class is Disability Claims Specialist (grade 19).

## Scheduled Rates (VERIFIED — CMS AFSCME RC-062, Regular Formula Rate, effective July 1, 2025)
IL pays monthly; annual = monthly × 12. The 7/1/2025 schedule has steps 1b,1a,1,2..8 (step 1c was eliminated in the 4% increase). min = step 1b, max = step 8.

| Class | Pay Grade | Monthly min | Monthly max | Annual min | Annual max |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Disability Claims Adjudicator Trainee (12539) | RC-062-13 | $4,756 | $6,533 | $57,072 | $78,396 |
| Disability Claims Adjudicator I (12537) | RC-062-16 | $5,384 | $7,631 | $64,608 | $91,572 |
| Disability Claims Adjudicator II (12538) | RC-062-18 | $5,928 | $8,493 | $71,136 | $101,916 |
| Disability Claims Specialist (12558) | RC-062-19 | $6,242 | $9,021 | $74,904 | $108,252 |

Both the grade assignment AND the dollar figures are from official IL sources → Authoritative.

## Methodology note (anti-fabrication / effective date)
The CMS RC-062 rates PDF contains FIVE effective-date schedules (7/1/2023, 1/1/2024, 7/1/2024, 7/1/2025, 7/1/2026 — successive AFSCME 2023-2027 raises). I used the **7/1/2025** page (the current schedule as of 2026-06-02); the 7/1/2026 page is a future schedule and was NOT used. Dollar figures were extracted from the downloaded PDF text locally with `pypdf` and the correct page was identified by its "EFFECTIVE …" header. Evidence: `.firecrawl/IL-rc062-rates.pdf`, `.firecrawl/IL-rc062-table.md`, scripts `_il_dates.py` / `_il_page4.py`.

## Bargaining Unit
- **AFSCME Council 31**, bargaining unit **RC-062** (Technical). Master Contract 2023-2027.

## Supervisor caveat
The RC-062 series tops out (for this card) at Disability Claims Specialist (grade 19). True first-line supervision in IDHS DDS may be a Public Service Administrator (PSA, merit-comp) title outside RC-062 and is not covered here; the Specialist is recorded as the senior in-series examiner-track class.

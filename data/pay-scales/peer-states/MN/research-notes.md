# Minnesota DDS Examiner & Supervisor Salary Research

## Administering Agency
**Minnesota Department of Employment and Economic Development (DEED), Disability Determination Services Division**
- Contact: https://mn.gov/deed/dds/
- Primary agency: DEED (not DHS; confirmed via official DEED DDS website)

## Examiner Classification

### Disability Examiner (Class Code 0871)
- **Class Code:** 0871
- **Class Title:** Disability Examiner
- **Bargaining Unit:** MAPE Unit 214 (Minnesota Association of Professional Employees)
- **Compensation Grid:** 14G
- **Compensation Code:** 07L
- **Step Basis:** Hourly rates (multiply by 2080 for annual full-time equivalent)

#### Salary Ranges (2023-2025 Contract, effective July 1, 2023)
- **Minimum:** $24.20/hour → $50,530 annual
- **Maximum:** $35.13/hour → $73,081 annual
- **Grade Structure:** Multi-step pay grade (step count not explicitly confirmed in accessible sources)

#### Salary Ranges (2025-2027 Contract, estimated)
Per MAPE 2025-2027 ratified contract:
- **July 1, 2025 increase:** +1.5% across-the-board
  - Minimum: $24.56/hour → $51,140 annual
  - Maximum: $35.66/hour → $74,174 annual
- **July 1, 2026 increase:** +1.75% additional
  - Minimum: $24.99/hour → $52,039 annual
  - Maximum: $36.28/hour → $75,502 annual
- **Step Increases:** Estimated 3.55% annually

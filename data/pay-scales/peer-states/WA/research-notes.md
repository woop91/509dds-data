## Supervisor class title

**DDS Adjudicator 5** serves as the first-line supervisor and expert level position.

**Duties include:**
- Supervises a work unit of disability adjudicators and support personnel
- Monitors and evaluates performance of subordinates
- Maintains and coordinates liaison with Social Security field offices and Community Service offices in assigned geographical areas
- Provides medical and policy review on case decisions by DDS Adjudicators
- Provides introductory and ongoing training for DDS Adjudicators and contracted Medical Consultants
- Considered experts with advanced knowledge of federal disability laws and policies

**Bargaining unit status:**
DDS Adjudicators (all levels including supervisory level) are represented by **Washington Federation of State Employees (WFSE) General Government bargaining unit**. Supervisors are included in the unit, not excluded as Washington Management Service.

## Pay grades and scheduled rates

### Salary Information (as of July 1, 2025)

**DDS Adjudicator 1 (Salary Range 46):**
- Monthly salary (Step L): $4,892
- Annual equivalent: ~$58,704

**DDS Adjudicator 2 (Salary Range 52):**
- Monthly salary (Step L): $5,673
- Annual equivalent: ~$68,076

**DDS Adjudicator 3 (Salary Range 56):**
- Monthly salary (Step L): $6,260
- Annual equivalent: ~$75,120

**DDS Adjudicator 5 (Salary Range not confirmed in research):**
- Estimated higher than Range 56 based on supervisory responsibilities

### WFSE General Government CBA (2025-2027)

**Agreement Terms:**
- Effective dates: 2025-2027 (ratified)
- General wage increases: 3% in 2025, 2% in 2026
- Starting wage for state employees raised to $18.00/hour
- More than 330 classification-specific increases achieved in this CBA
- Health care premium share maintained at 85%/15%
- FSA benefit increased to $300 for employees earning $68,004/year or less

## Data gaps and limitations

1. **DDS Adjudicator 5 pay range not confirmed:** Research identified this as the supervisory level but the specific salary range (likely Range 62 or higher) was not directly verified in accessed sources.

2. **Full salary step data not accessed:** Only Step L (highest step) values were found. Complete step A-L progression for each range would provide fuller compensation picture.

3. **Collective bargaining unit confirmation limited:** Confirmed WFSE GG unit covers examiners; supervisory level inclusion in the unit (vs. WMS exclusion) inferred but not explicitly confirmed in accessed documents.

4. **Public payroll database not queried:** Washington's fiscal.wa.gov state payroll database exists and contains historical salary data but was not directly accessed in this research.

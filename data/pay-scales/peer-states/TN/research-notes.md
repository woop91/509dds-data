# Tennessee DDS Pay Scale Research Notes

## Administering agency

- **Full name:** Tennessee Department of Human Services (TDHS), Division of Rehabilitation Services, Disability Determination Services (DDS)
- **Source:** https://www.tn.gov/humanservices/ds/disability-determination-services.html

The Tennessee DDS operates by agreement between the State of Tennessee and the Social Security Administration to process Social Security and Supplemental Security Income disability claims.

---

## Examiner class title

- **Official classification:** Disability Claims Examiner (DCE)
- **Alternative titles used:** Disability Examiner, Disability Determination Specialist
- **Class code:** Not publicly accessible; requires request to Tennessee Department of Human Resources (TDHR)
- **Class specification URL:** Class specification documents exist on tn.gov but PDFs were not directly accessible during research:
  - [DHS SR Disability Claims Examiner - DDS Staff (November 2024)](https://www.tn.gov/content/dam/tn/human-services/documents/HRC0012192_DHS_SR_Disability_Claims_Exmr_Davidson_111124.pdf)
  - [Disability Claims Examiner - Robertson County posting (June 2023)](https://www.tn.gov/content/dam/tn/human-services/documents/Disability_Claims_Examiner_Robertson_061423.pdf)
- **Bargaining unit status:** Tennessee is a right-to-work state; most state employees are not covered by collective bargaining agreements

---

## Supervisor class title

- **Official title:** Not definitively identified
- **Most likely titles:** Operations Manager, Supervisor (disability examiner operations), Regional Director
- **Class code:** Not publicly accessible
- **Status:** No specific class specification URL found for first-line supervisor role

Job postings reference multiple supervisory/management progression levels but specific first-line supervisor title requires direct contact with TDHS HR.

## Pay grade + scheduled rates

### Disability Claims Examiner

- **Estimated total compensation:** $75,813–$79,699 annually (State of Tennessee Department of Human Services)
  - Base pay estimate: $75,813
  - Estimated total with benefits: $79,699
- **Alternative salary estimate:** $56,959 (general State of Tennessee positions)
- **Hourly equivalent (market rate):** ~$26.17/hour (December 2024)
- **Pay grade:** Not publicly listed; accessible via Tennessee state HR compensation plan document only

### Supervisor (estimated)

- **Estimated salary range:** ~$74,705 annually (average supervisor in Tennessee state government)
- **Pay grade:** Not determined

### Official salary schedule

- **State compensation plan:** https://www.tn.gov/hr/hr-business-partners/classification-compensation/redirect-classification-compensation-plan.html
- **Detailed compensation plan PDF:** https://www.tn.gov/content/dam/tn/hr/documents/AlphaCompPlan.pdf (contains job code, salary plan, and salary grade fields but was not accessible for content extraction)
- **Interactive salary search:** https://salary.app.tn.gov/public/searchsalary (State Employee Salary Search tool)

## Research constraints

**PDF documents not accessible:** Class specification PDFs and the full compensation plan PDF were blocked during scraping attempts. Specific pay grades, class codes, and detailed job description requirements could not be extracted.

**Public information gaps:** Tennessee does not publish detailed salary schedules or pay grade mappings for individual classifications on public-facing web pages. This information is:
- Available in the full compensation plan document (PDF)
- Searchable via the state employee salary search application
- Available by direct request to Tennessee Department of Human Resources

**Contact for complete information:**
- Tennessee DDS: Phone 800-342-1117 or P.O. Box 775, Nashville, TN 37202
- Tennessee Department of Human Resources (TDHR): https://www.tn.gov/hr

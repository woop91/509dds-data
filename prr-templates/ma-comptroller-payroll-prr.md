**Subject:** Public Records Request — Archival HRCMS payroll for Disability
Determination Services examiner titles, CY2001–CY2009

**Body:**

Pursuant to M.G.L. c. 66, § 10, I request the following **archival payroll
records** for employees of the **Massachusetts Rehabilitation Commission (MRC)**
— department code historically used for MRC/MassAbility — whose position title
matches the Disability Determination Services examiner series:

1. For **each calendar year 2001 through 2009**, a per-employee, per-year extract
   from HRCMS for MRC employees whose `position_title` (or equivalent field)
   contains **"DISAB EXAMINER"** (i.e., Vocational Disability Examiner, all
   grades — A/B/C/D and I/II/III as titled in those years), including for each
   record the fields that CTHRU publishes for later years:
   - employee name
   - position title and pay grade
   - bargaining unit
   - annual / scheduled rate
   - regular pay paid (year-to-date or annual)
   - overtime pay paid
   - other pay (differentials, stipends)
   - buyout pay
   - total pay
2. If a clean title-filtered extract is burdensome, the **full MRC departmental
   payroll** for CY2001–CY2009 in the same column layout is an acceptable
   substitute; I will filter it myself.

This mirrors the public CTHRU dataset `rxhc-k6iz` so the older years can be
appended to the existing 2010–2023 series with an identical schema.

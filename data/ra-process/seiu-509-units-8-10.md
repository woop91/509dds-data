# SEIU Local 509 (Units 8 and 10)

SEIU Local 509 - Commonwealth Human Service Workers and Educators Training and Professional Development Fund provides comprehensive training opportunities for SEIU Local 509 bargaining unit employees.

The Training trust website - [www.509training.org](https://urldefense.com/v3/__http:/www.509training.org__;!!CPANwP4y!TgUBrcskN_BKEffrk8a4QWgY_MbUcG5paksxZBm1ei-kBLPuP6QJIRx2cR7cb5_9J0504xyrTma-XXu9xyRXF21CwLSHsdA$) \- Provides full details of all of programs, including registration forms, as well as application information for grant programs, and links to asynchronous offerings.

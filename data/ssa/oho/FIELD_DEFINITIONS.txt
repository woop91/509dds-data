# OHO DATA FIELDS REFERENCE
Based on SSA Data Dictionary (https://www.ssa.gov/appeals/DataSets/data_dictionary.html)

## 1. ALJ DISPOSITION DATA (03_ALJ_Disposition_Data)
Fields:
- ALJ_NM25: Administrative Law Judge Name (CHAR 25)
- OFFICE: Office Name (CHAR 25) - owning hearing office
- DSPN_CNT: Total Dispositions (INTEGER) - actions completed during period
- TOTAL_ALJ_DISPOSITIONS_ACROSS_ALL_OFFICES: Sum across all offices where ALJ worked (INTEGER)
- DCN_CNT: Decision Count (INTEGER) - fully favorable + partially favorable + unfavorable
- TOTAW: Total Awards (INTEGER) - fully favorable + partially favorable
- FFVRBL_DCN_CNT: Fully Favorable Decision Count (INTEGER)
- PFVRBL_DCN_CNT: Partially Favorable Decision Count (INTEGER)
- UFDCN_CNT: Unfavorable Decision Count (INTEGER)

Period: Monthly archives available FY2011-FY2025
Latest: FY2025 YTD (09/28/2024 - 09/26/2025)

## 2. HEARING OFFICE WORKLOAD DATA (02_HO_Workload_Data)
Fields:
- OFFICE: Hearing Office Name (CHAR 25)
- RECEIPTS: Count of adjusted receipts during period (INTEGER) - can be negative if transfers exceed new receipts
- DSPN_CNT: Dispositions/completions (INTEGER)
- PEND_END_CNT: Cases pending at end of period (INTEGER)
- DSPN_AVGPT: Average Processing Time (SMALLINT) - calendar days from request to disposition

Period: Monthly archives FY2011-FY2025
Latest: FY2025 YTD (09/28/2024 - 09/26/2025)
Note: Year-aggregated view only (no time-series monthly breakdown in current report)

## 3. AVERAGE WAIT TIME UNTIL HEARING HELD (01_NetStat_Report)
Fields:
- HO_CODE: Hearing Site Unique Identifier (CHAR 4)
- OFFICE: Hearing Office Name
- HO_TIMES: Months from hearing request to hearing held (days/30) (INTEGER)
- MONTH: Reporting month (DATE)

Period: Monthly archives FY2011-FY2025
Latest: FY2025 YTD
Time-series: YES - monthly data available

## 4. ALJ DISPOSITION RANKING (04_Disposition_Per_Day_Per_ALJ_Ranking_Report)
Fields:
- RANK: Ranking among all offices (INTEGER 1-160+)
- OFFICE: Hearing Office Name
- RONUM: Region Number (CHAR 2)
- DLY_DSPN_ALJ_ACNT: Dispositions per ALJ per workday (DECIMAL 5.2)
- WRKDAYS: Work days in period (SMALLINT) - e.g., 247 for FY2025 YTD

Period: Monthly archives FY2011-FY2025
Latest: FY2025 YTD
Note: Office-level averages only, not individual ALJ rates

## 5. HEARING OFFICE AVERAGE PROCESSING TIME RANKING (05_Average_Processing_Time_Report)
Fields:
- RANK: Ranking among all offices (INTEGER)
- OFFICE: Hearing Office Name
- RONUM: Region Number (CHAR 2)
- DSPN_AVGPT: Average processing time in days (SMALLINT)

Period: Monthly archives FY2011-FY2025
Latest: FY2025 YTD
Time-series: YES - monthly ranking data

## DATA AVAILABILITY NOTES
- All datasets available as JavaScript-rendered HTML tables
- XML export available for each report (links: https://www.ssa.gov/appeals/DataSets/NN_Report.xml)
- Monthly archives extend back to FY2011
- No time-series monthly breakdown for workload/disposition data in main reports
- ALJ data includes note: factors like management duties, part-time status, leave not accounted for

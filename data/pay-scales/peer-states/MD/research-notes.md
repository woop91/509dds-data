# Maryland DDS Salary Research Notes

## Administering Agency
- **Full Agency Name:** Maryland State Department of Education (MSDE), Division of Rehabilitation Services (DORS), Disability Determination Services Branch
- **Source:** https://dors.maryland.gov/resources/Pages/DDS.aspx

## Examiner Class Title

### Disability Claims Examiner I (Entry Level)
- **Official Maryland State Classification:** Disability Claims Examiner I
- **Class Code:** 5260
- **Pay Grade:** ASTD14 (Grade 14)
- **Salary Range:** $58,428.00 – $90,709.00 per year (based on job posting; specific FY 2026 schedule rates not accessible via PDF)
- **Class Specification URL:** https://www.jobapscloud.com/MD/specs/classspecdisplay.asp?ClassNumber=005260

### Disability Claims Examiner II (Advanced Level)
- **Official Title:** Disability Claims Examiner II
- **Class Code:** Referenced in job specifications but salary data not captured
- **Note:** Differs from Examiner I by degree of supervisory control; performs full range of duties under general supervision versus close supervision

## Supervisor Class Title

### Disability Claims Examiner Supervisor
- **Official Title:** Disability Claims Examiner Supervisor
- **Class Code:** 5263
- **Pay Grade:** Grade 19
- **Salary Range:** $80,541.00 – $125,564.00 per year
- **Class Specification URL:** https://www.jobapscloud.com/MD/sup/BulPreview.asp?R1=25&R2=005263&R3=0001
- **Minimum Qualifications:** Bachelor's degree + 4 years professional experience in Social Security disability adjudication (2 years minimum as Disability Claims Technical Examiner)

## Bargaining Unit / Collective Bargaining Status

- **Status:** Maryland state employees represented by American Federation of State, County and Municipal Employees (AFSCME)
- **Union Coverage:** Disability Claims Examiner positions appear to be covered under AFSCME collective bargaining for non-supervisory positions; Disability Claims Examiner Supervisor positions are represented by AFSCME Maryland Supervisors Union (Unit S)
- **Contract:** State-level AFSCME contract effective through December 31, 2026
- **Notes:** Specific bargaining unit assignment (Unit C vs. other units) could not be definitively confirmed from publicly available sources; recommend contacting Maryland DBM Division of Classification and Salary for clarification

# Connecticut (CT) DDS Salary Research

## Administering Agency
**Connecticut Department of Social Services (DSS), Disability Determination Services Unit**
- Part of the state's disability determination system under Social Security Administration (SSA) cooperative agreement
- Operated as a separate bureau within DSS

## Examiner Classification
- **Class Title:** Disability Determination Examiner (or Adjudicator)
- **CT DAS Classification System:** Reviewed via CT Department of Administrative Services (DAS) class specification database
- **Salary Group (SG):** Approximately SG-21 to SG-23 (typical for DDS examiner roles in CT P-2 unit)
- **Pay Grade:** Classified under AFSCME Local 714 P-2 (Social and Human Services) unit

## Supervisor Classification  
- **Class Title:** Disability Determination Examiner Supervisor (or Supervisor, Disability Determination)
- **Salary Group (SG):** Approximately SG-24 to SG-26 (first-line supervisor level in P-3 supervisory unit)
- **Unit:** SEBAC P-3 (Supervisory and Professional) or separate supervisory bargaining unit

## Bargaining Status
- **Union:** AFSCME Council 4, Local 714
- **Bargaining Unit:** P-2 (Social and Human Services) — examiners
- **CBA Term:** 2017 → 2027 (long-term SEBAC agreement)
- **Step System:** 9-step salary progression (typical for CT state positions)

## Pay Grades & Scheduled Rates
Connecticut uses a "Salary Group" (SG) classification system with annual step progression. Exact rates are published in the CT DAS Salary Plans, typically updated annually for July 1 effective date.

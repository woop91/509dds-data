## The Details

### What you should know

- A public employee is anyone who works for a state, county, or municipal entity.
- Police, fire, and EMS departments are requested to report injuries that occur to public sector employees.
- Serious incidents—such as amputations, loss of an eye, hospitalization, or fatalities—require immediate reporting.

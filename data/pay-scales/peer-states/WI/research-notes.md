# Wisconsin DDS Pay Scale Research

## Administering agency
- Full agency name: **Wisconsin Department of Health Services (DHS)**, Division of Medicaid Services (DMS) → **Disability Determination Bureau (DDB)**
- Source: [Wisconsin Disability Determination Bureau | DHS](https://www.dhs.wisconsin.gov/ddb/index.htm)

## Examiner class title
- Official Wisconsin state classification title: **Disability Determination Specialist**
- Class code: **49201**
- Wisconsin Division of Personnel Management (DPM) class specification URL: https://dpm.wi.gov/Specs/49201SPC.pdf
  - *Note: PDF is digitally encoded and content could not be fully extracted via web tools*
- Bargaining unit / collective bargaining status: **Non-represented classified position** post-Wisconsin Act 10 (2011)
  - Wisconsin Act 10 eliminated most state-employee collective bargaining in 2011; compensation for non-represented classified employees is set by the Legislature on a biennial basis rather than through negotiation

## Supervisor class title
- Official title for first-line supervisor: **Disability Determination Supervisor**
- Class code: **49220**
- Related mid-level manager title: **Disability Determination Program Manager** (class code 49240)
- Class specification URLs:
  - Supervisor (49220): https://dpm.wi.gov/Specs/49220SPC.pdf
  - Program Manager (49240): https://dpm.wi.gov/Specs/49240SPC.pdf

### What was found:
- The 2025-27 Wisconsin Compensation Plan includes a "pay progression" for Disability Determination Specialist positions with $1.00 differentials at 5, 7, and 10 years of service
- General wage adjustments (GWA) for 2025-27: 3.0% effective August 10, 2025, and 2.0% effective June 28, 2026
- Market-rate salary data (non-official): Disability Determination Specialist roles in Wisconsin average $60,202/year, with ranges $48,400–$65,600 (as of April 2026, per ZipRecruiter)

## Recommended next steps

1. **Contact DPM directly** at DOADPMClassificationGenInfo@wisconsin.gov or DOADPMCompEmploymentRelGenInfo@wisconsin.gov to request readable copies of class specs and current pay schedules for codes 49201, 49220, and 49240
2. **Access OpenBook Wisconsin** (openbook.wi.gov) to search current and historical payroll data for DHS DDB employees by class code
3. **Review JCOER meeting minutes** (Joint Committee on Employment Relations) from recent sessions for compensation plan adoption documents with detailed pay tables
4. **Query the Employee Relations System (ERS)** via ers.state.wi.us for the authoritative class code pay range assignments

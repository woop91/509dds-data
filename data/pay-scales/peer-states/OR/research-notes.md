# Oregon DDS Salary Research

## Administering Agency
Oregon Department of Human Services (DHS), Disability Determination Services
- Salem office: 3150 Lancaster Dr NE, Salem OR 97305-1350
- Phone: 503-986-5100 or 800-452-2147

## Examiner Class Title
**Disability Analyst 1** (primary entry-level classification)
- Job Profile Code: 5926
- Category: Regulation, Enforcement, and Investigation

**Disability Analyst 2** (advanced/experienced level)
- Job Profile Code: 5927
- Category: Regulation, Enforcement, and Investigation

Note: Oregon's state HR classification system uses "Disability Analyst" rather than "Disability Examiner" or "Claims Adjudicator."

## Supervisor/Manager Class Title
**Disability Analysis and Adjudication Manager 3**
- Job Profile Code: 7193
- Category: Principal, Executive, Manager and Other Unclassified Titles

## Pay Grade and Scheduled Rates

**Data retrieval challenge:** Oregon DAS Classification and Compensation system (apps.oregon.gov/DAS/Classification-Compensation) hosts the official salary schedules but the specific pay band ranges and step progressions are not directly published in machine-readable format on public web pages. The salary information is interactive via the Oregon DAS portal.

From available sources:
- **Disability Analyst average salary (2025):** ~$77,458/year ($37.24/hour per ZipRecruiter)
- **Entry-level range:** Estimated $50,000–$65,000/year (market data)
- **Supervisor/Manager range:** Estimated $85,000–$110,000/year (market data)

**Effective date:** Salary schedules updated effective January 1, 2025; 2.5% COLA effective February 1, 2026; 4% COLA scheduled January 1, 2027.

## Bargaining Unit and Union Representation
**SEIU Local 503 / Oregon Public Employees Union (OPEU)**
- DHS employees covered under **DHS Coalition Agreement**
- Current contract period: **2023–2025** (expires June 30, 2025)
- Successor contract negotiations: 2025–2027 bargaining commenced March 2025
- Union affiliation: Service Employees International Union (SEIU) Local 503

## Data Gaps

1. **Specific pay band codes** (e.g., OPS21): Oregon DAS does not publish these in accessible format; requires direct portal access or FOIA request
2. **Step progression detail:** Number of steps and increment amounts not publicly available
3. **Supervisor exact salary range:** Manager 3 classification exists but detailed range not found
4. **Current contract effective salary schedule:** 2023–2025 Human Services Coalition CBA appendices not fully accessible via web scrape

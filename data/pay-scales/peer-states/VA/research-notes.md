# Virginia DDS Examiner and Supervisor Salary Research

## Administering Agency

- **Full agency name + division**: Virginia Department for Aging and Rehabilitative Services (DARS), Disability Determination Services Division
- **Source URL**: https://dars.virginia.gov/benefits/disability-determination/

## Examiner Class Title

- **Official Virginia state classification title**: DDS Analyst (levels: Trainee 1, Journey, Junior)
  - Role is classified under Virginia's standard state pay band system (not a specific "Disability Adjudicator" title, but functionally equivalent)
- **Role codes** (from jobs.virginia.gov):
  - DDS Analyst – Trainee 1 Level: #ARSD0134
  - DDS Analyst - Journey Level: #ARSD0315
  - DDS Analyst – Junior Level: #ARSD0436
- **Virginia DHRM compensation plan / class spec URL**: 
  - [Compensation and Classification](https://www.dhrm.virginia.gov/hr-partners/compensation-and-classification)
  - [Classified Salary Structure Effective June 10, 2025](https://www.dhrm.virginia.gov/docs/default-source/compensationdocuments/fy26salarystructure.pdf)
- **Bargaining unit / collective bargaining status**: Virginia does NOT have collective bargaining for state employees (verified as of 2026)

## Supervisor Class Title

- **Official role title for first-line supervisor**: DDS Supervisor - Line Unit
- **Role code**: #ARSD0252
- **Class spec URL**: [DHRM Compensation and Classification](https://www.dhrm.virginia.gov/hr-partners/compensation-and-classification)

## Pay Grade + Scheduled Rates

### DDS Analyst (Examiner Role)

| Level | Pay Band | Starting Salary (2026) | Notes |
|-------|----------|----------------------|-------|
| Trainee 1 | 4 | $54,106 | Entry-level, no prior experience required, paid training provided |
| Journey | 4 | $64,190 | Mid-level, requires demonstrated competency |
| Junior | NV 4 | $69,479 | Higher entry point with additional qualifications |

### DDS Supervisor - Line Unit

| Classification | Pay Band | Starting Salary (2026) |
|----------------|----------|----------------------|
| Supervisor | NV 5 | $105,212 |

**Fiscal Year**: Information effective June 10, 2025 (FY 2026 and ongoing)

**Official Pay Band Schedule**: Virginia uses a classified pay band system with bands ranging from basic to senior levels. The June 10, 2025 salary structure applied a 3% increase to both Statewide and NOVA pay bands.

**URL to Virginia's official pay plan**: 
- [DHRM Pay and Holiday Calendar](https://www.dhrm.virginia.gov/payandholidaycalendar)
- [DHRM Jobs and Salary Structure](https://www.dhrm.virginia.gov/jobs-and-careers/jobs-and-salary-structure)

## Additional Context

Virginia DDS uses a **state-specific internal salary model** separate from the general DHRM pay band schedule:
- The [Virginia Disability Determination Services Salary Model](https://www.vadds.virginia.gov/downloads/Virginia%20Disability%20Determination%20Services%20Salary%20Model.pdf) documents internal step progression
- The model Q&As (updated 3/15/2023) reference supervisor progression from F1 ($84,546) to F4 ($93,000), but these may represent legacy or internal classifications distinct from the current DHRM NV 5 pay band

# Pennsylvania DDS Examiner Salary Research

**Last Updated:** 2026-05-08

## Administering Agency
- **Department:** Pennsylvania Department of Labor & Industry (DLI), Bureau of Disability Determination
- **Jurisdiction:** Social Security Disability Determination for Pennsylvania

## Examiner Class Titles
- Primary: **Disability Examiner I** (entry-level, SC-09 range)
- Advanced: **Disability Examiner II** (experienced, SC-09 to SC-10 range)
- **Trainee:** Disability Examiner Trainee (SC-08, training period ~6 mo)

*Note: Pennsylvania uses the Standard Compensation (SC) scale for classified state civil service positions.*

## Supervisor Class Title
- **Medical-Vocational Supervisor** or **Disability Examiner Supervisor** (SC-11 to SC-12 range)

## Bargaining Unit & Union

- **Status:** Examiners are covered state classified employees under the master CBA

# New York DDS Salary Research Notes

## Administering Agency
**New York Office of Temporary and Disability Assistance (OTDA), Division of Disability Determinations (DDD)**

OTDA is the cabinet-level agency responsible for disability determination services in New York State. The DDD operates as the disability determination unit within OTDA, processing Social Security Disability Insurance (SSDI) and Supplemental Security Income (SSI) claims on behalf of the Social Security Administration.

## Examiner Classification

The entry and mid-level examiner position is classified as **Disability Analyst** (verified job title used in OTDA postings and civil service registers).

NY civil service classifies Disability Analyst positions across three tiers:
- **Disability Analyst (Trainee)** — Entry-level, post-initial appointment to permanent position
- **Disability Analyst 1** — Permanent competitive class, minimum 1-2 years experience
- **Disability Analyst 2** — Higher-level, advanced responsibility tier

(Note: As of May 2026, exact Salary Grade (SG) numbers for each tier were not accessible via WebFetch or firecrawl due to HTML paywall restrictions on cs.ny.gov and network connectivity issues with openbook.ny.gov. See "Data Gaps" below.)

## Supervisor Classification

First-line supervisors are typically classified under:
- **Disability Analyst Supervisor** or **Supervising Disability Analyst**
- May also appear as **Program Specialist** or **Project Manager** roles within OTDA

(Exact grade mapping requires access to NY Civil Service Commission Job Title and Salary Schedule documents.)

## Pay Grade Structure

New York uses a **Salary Grade (SG)** system ranging SG 8–SG 27+ across all bargaining units. The PS&T (Professional, Scientific & Technical) unit salary schedule defines pay bands with step increments (typically 9 steps per grade).

### Salary Schedule Characteristics
- **Basis**: Annual salary, 12-month pay period
- **Step progression**: Annual step increments (steps 1–9 for most grades)
- **Effective date**: Last known schedule update effective July 1, 2025 (per PEF contract history)
- **Raise schedule**: April 2025 raise (4% per 2023–2026 collective bargaining agreement)

**Action required**: To obtain SG numbers and min/max salary figures, direct access to the official **NY Department of Civil Service Job Title and Salary Schedule (PS&T Unit)** published at cs.ny.gov is needed. The document is typically titled "Professional, Scientific and Technical (PS&T) Unit Salary Schedule (Effective 07-01-25)" or similar.

## Bargaining Unit Details

**Bargaining Unit**: PEF Bargaining Unit 5 — Professional, Scientific & Technical (PS&T)

**Union**: Public Employees Federation (PEF) — American Federation of State, County and Municipal Employees (AFSCME) affiliate

**Current CBA**: 
- Effective dates: April 2, 2023 – April 1, 2026
- Status: Currently in effect (expires April 1, 2026)
- Key provision: 4% annual raise April 2025

**PEF Membership**: OTDA Disability Analysts are covered civil service employees under PEF Unit 5 PS&T. The CBA applies to all salary and benefit adjustments.

**Salary Schedule Resource**: PEF maintains official salary charts at pef.org under "Salary Schedule" or "Contracts" sections; the April 2025 schedule should reflect the 4% raise across all steps and grades.

# Indiana DDS Salary Research Notes

## Administering agency

- **Full agency name:** Indiana Family and Social Services Administration (FSSA), Disability Determination Bureau (DDB)
- **Source URL:** https://www.in.gov/fssa/disability-services/disability-determination-bureau/

## Examiner class title

- **Official Indiana state classification title:** Disability Claims Adjudicator
- **Class code:** Not definitively identified in public sources; referenced in job postings on WorkforIndiana portal
- **Indiana State Personnel Department class specification URL:** Class specification document not publicly available; would require direct request to FSSA DDB or SPD
- **Bargaining unit / collective bargaining status:** Indiana ended state-employee collective bargaining in 2005 by executive order (Executive Order 15-13); Disability Claims Adjudicators are non-union civil service positions

## Supervisor class title

- **Official title for first-line supervisor:** Not definitively identified in public sources. References suggest "Lead Workers" manage units, but no formal state civil service classification found.
- **Class code:** Not available
- **Class spec URL:** Not available

## Research gaps & limitations

1. **Specific pay grade assignment:** Indiana's official salary schedule PDF lists many job codes but does not explicitly map "Disability Claims Adjudicator" to a particular grade. Contact FSSA DDB or Indiana SPD for definitive classification.

2. **Supervisor title & compensation:** No formal state civil service job classification found for disability examiner supervisors. "Lead Workers" mentioned in employee reviews but not in official SPD documentation. May be informal or titled differently.

3. **Class specification documents:** Individual job class specifications (detailing duties, requirements, qualifications) for both examiner and supervisor positions are not publicly accessible online. Require direct request to:
   - Indiana State Personnel Department: https://www.in.gov/spd/
   - FSSA Disability Determination Bureau: DDB.inquiries@fssa.in.gov

4. **Indiana Transparency Portal:** ITP payroll data (itp.in.gov) does not appear to be indexed or searchable by job title; raw payroll data may be available via FOIA request.

5. **Collective bargaining status:** Indiana ended state-employee collective bargaining in 2005; confirmed non-union status, but no formal labor agreements govern examiner/supervisor compensation as of 2025.

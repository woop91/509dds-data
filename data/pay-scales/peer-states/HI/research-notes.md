## Scheduled Rates (VERIFIED — DHRD BU 13 salary schedule, effective 07/01/2025)
HI uses the Salary Range (SR) system; the BU13 (non-supervisory) schedule prints steps D–M. min = Step D, max = Step M (annual):

| Class | SR | Min (Step D) | Max (Step M) |
|---|---|---|---|
| Disability Claims Specialist II (3.386) | SR-18 | $55,080 | $78,420 |
| Disability Claims Specialist III (3.387) | SR-20 | $59,532 | $84,828 |
| Disability Claims Specialist IV (3.388, supervisor) | SR-22 (candidate) | $64,428 | $91,728 |

For DCS II and III, both the SR assignment AND the dollar figures are from official HI sources → Authoritative. DCS IV's SR-22 is a candidate (schedule values shown, grade unverified).

## Hiring-range vs schedule-range note
HI postings quote a *hiring* sub-range (e.g. DCS II posting: "$4,339 to $5,282 per month, SR-18, Steps C to I"). The full salary range recorded here uses the official non-supervisory BU13 schedule's published steps D (min) through M (max). The posting's monthly hiring figures are consistent with the SR-18 schedule (e.g. Step D $55,080/yr = $4,590/mo).

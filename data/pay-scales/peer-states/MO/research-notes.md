# Missouri DDS Pay Scale Research Notes

## Administering Agency

**Agency Name:** Missouri Department of Elementary and Secondary Education (DESE), Office of Adult Learning and Rehabilitation Services

**Clarification:** Missouri Disability Determination Services (DDS) is NOT administered by the Department of Social Services (DSS) Family Support Division. It is instead housed within DESE's Office of Adult Learning and Rehabilitation Services division.

**Federal Funding:** The program is "fully funded by the federal government" via the Social Security Administration (SSA) and determines medical eligibility for Missourians who have filed for disability benefits (SSDI/SSI).

**Source URL:** [https://dese.mo.gov/adult-learning-rehabilitation-services/disability-determination](https://dese.mo.gov/adult-learning-rehabilitation-services/disability-determination)

## Examiner Class Title

**Official Title:** Not definitively located in public records

**Finding:** Missouri DESE DDS does not appear to use a standardized state job classification title published on the Office of Administration (OA) personnel website. The public "Work with Us" page mentions recruitment for "DD Counselor" positions only. State job class specifications for disability examiners were not found in searches of oa.mo.gov/personnel/classification-specifications.

**Closest Related Positions:** 
- Missouri OA Classification #0866: **Disability Program Representative** (Pay Grade 06, $36,360–$57,792 annually)
- Missouri OA Classification #0868: **Disability Program Specialist** (Pay Grade 07, $41,208–$65,544 annually)

These appear to serve the Governor's Council on Disability, not DESE DDS directly.

**Barrier:** DDS examiner positions may be classified under DESE's own internal classification system rather than the state-wide OA uniform classification grid, or specific class codes may not be publicly indexed in searchable form.

**Source URLs:**
- DESE DDS careers: [https://dese.mo.gov/work-us-disability-determination](https://dese.mo.gov/work-us-disability-determination)
- OA Disability Program Representative spec: [https://oa.mo.gov/personnel/classification-specifications/0866](https://oa.mo.gov/personnel/classification-specifications/0866)
- OA Disability Program Specialist spec: [https://oa.mo.gov/personnel/classification-specifications/0868](https://oa.mo.gov/personnel/classification-specifications/0868)

## Supervisor Class Title

**Official Title:** Not located

**Finding:** No first-line supervisor or lead examiner class title was found in public Missouri state job classification records for DDS supervision roles.

## Pay Grade & Scheduled Rates

**Status:** Unable to determine

**Challenge:** Missouri transitioned to a "Uniform Classification and Pay System Pay Plan" administered by the Missouri Office of Administration, Division of Personnel. While this system maintains downloadable compensation structure documents (General, IT, Medical, Nursing) and a searchable "Class Occupational Details" spreadsheet keyed by Job Class and Pay Grade, the specific pay grades for DESE DDS examiner and supervisor positions are not accessible through public web searches.

**Available Resources:**
- OA Uniform Classification and Pay System documents: [https://pers.oa.mo.gov/uniform-classification-and-pay-system-pay-plan](https://pers.oa.mo.gov/uniform-classification-and-pay-system-pay-plan)
- Missouri Accountability and Transparency (MAP) salary portal: [https://mapyourtaxes.mo.gov/map/employees/Agency/Agencies.aspx](https://mapyourtaxes.mo.gov/map/employees/Agency/Agencies.aspx)

**Note on 2022 Classification Reform:** Missouri did conduct a statewide classification reform in 2022 that consolidated grades into a "Total Compensation" pay grid, but specific allocation of DDS positions to the new grid structure has not been made public in searchable form.

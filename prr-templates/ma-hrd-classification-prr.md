**Subject:** Public Records Request — Vocational Disability Examiner
classification, reclassification, and authorized establishment

**Body:**

Pursuant to M.G.L. c. 66, § 10, I request the following classification records for
the **Vocational Disability Examiner (VDE)** series and related Disability
Determination Services titles (SEIU Local 509, Units 8 and 10):

1. The current **class specification(s)** for the Vocational Disability Examiner
   series, all grades/levels, and for the DDS first-line supervisor title.
2. All records documenting the **2016 reclassification** of the examiner series in
   which the A/B/C/D grade titles were converted to the I/II/III titles —
   including the reclassification request, HRD's determination, the crosswalk of
   old grade → new grade, and the effective date.
3. The **authorized establishment / position count** (authorized FTE) for the VDE
   series and for the DDS division as a whole, by title and grade, for each fiscal
   year for which HRD maintains it (please state the earliest year available).
4. Any **classification or compensation studies** HRD has conducted or
   commissioned concerning the Disability Determination Services examiner series,
   including any comparison to examiner classifications in other states.

## Public-interest note (voluntary)

The class specifications, the 2016 reclassification crosswalk, and the authorized
establishment counts are needed to interpret the published payroll-headcount data
(the 2016 reclass year inflates raw CTHRU counts because employees appear under
both naming conventions) and to document the long-run decline in authorized
examiner positions. Open dataset: github.com/Woop91/509dds-data.

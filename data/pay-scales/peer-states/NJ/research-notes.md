# New Jersey DDS Salary Research — Summary & Notes

## Administering Agency
- **State Agency:** New Jersey Department of Labor and Workforce Development (NJDOL)
- **Division:** Division of Disability Determination Services (DDS)
- **Location:** Operates Social Security Disability Insurance (SSDI) and Supplemental Security Income (SSI) determinations for NJ

## Examiner Class Titles (Verified)
- **Examiner Title:** Claims Adjudicator (entry/mid-level); Senior Claims Adjudicator (supervisory/senior)
- **Job Spec Codes:**
  - Senior Claims Adjudicator: CSC Job Spec #64947 (and bilingual variant 64947C)
  - Claims Adjudicator: (entry-level equivalent — spec ID not yet confirmed)
- **Supervisor Title:** Claims Adjudicator Supervisor (confirmed in Job Spec #64947 description)

## Bargaining Unit & Union Representation
- **Likely Union:** CWA Local 1036 (Communications Workers of America)
  - Covers broad NJ state executive branch; confirmed salary contract effective July 1, 2023 — June 30, 2027
- **Alternate Possibility:** IFPTE Local 195 (International Federation of Professional and Technical Engineers)
  - Covers ~10,000 NJ state employees; 2023-2027 contract ratified with 14.82% compounded wage increase over 4 years (3.5% annual)
  - Cannot confirm from available sources whether DDS Claims Adjudicators are under CWA 1036 or IFPTE 195 jurisdiction

## Pay Grade & Salary Structure
- **NJ Pay System:** Uses "Range" + "Step" structure (analogous to federal GS scale)
  - Range: primary grade classification (e.g., Range 22, Range 25, Range 28)
  - Step: seniority increment within range (Steps 1–10 standard; Step 11 added in 2023–2027 contract)
- **Confirmed Salary Range (Glassdoor data, State of NJ Claims Adjudicator positions):**
  - Minimum: ~$53,560/year (entry step)
  - Maximum: ~$68,777/year (highest step; pre-Step 11 addition)
  - **Note:** These figures reflect civilian/non-union sources and may not align exactly with CWA/IFPTE contractual rates
- **Scheduled Rates (CWA 1036, 2023–2027):**
  - PDF source: https://cwa1031.org/wp-content/uploads/2023/10/2023-2027-CWA-NJ-State-Salary-Schedules-1.pdf
  - **Status:** PDF not accessible via web-fetch tools; raw content unreadable. Salary tables exist but could not be extracted.

## CWA 2023–2027 Contract Highlights
- **Effective Dates:** July 1, 2023 — June 30, 2027
- **Wage Increases:** 3.5% annual increase (compounded)
- **New Benefit:** Step 11 created, raising maximum salaries for all CWA-represented state employees
- **Minimum Salary New Hire Floor:** Employees hired on/after July 1, 2024, start at no lower than Range 9, Step 2
- **Healthcare:** No givebacks; strongest compensation package in 15+ years per union announcement

## IFPTE 2023–2027 Contract Highlights (if applicable)
- **Ratification:** 96% member approval
- **Total Compounded Wage Increase:** 14.82% over 4 years
- **Annual Increases:** 3.5% each year
- **Key Change:** Elimination of pay cap at Step 10; workers advance beyond previous maximum

## Data Gaps & Limitations
1. **Union Jurisdiction Unconfirmed:** Could not definitively establish whether NJ DDS Claims Adjudicators are under CWA Local 1036 or IFPTE Local 195 from public sources. Direct contact with NJDOL HR or union locals required.
2. **Exact Pay Ranges by Job Code:** While Glassdoor aggregates civilian NJ salary data (~$53.5k–$68.8k), the precise Range/Step assignment for Claims Adjudicator and Senior Claims Adjudicator under the state's civil service classification is not publicly documented in accessible format.
3. **Supervisor Pay:** Senior Claims Adjudicator confirmed as supervisory title; exact pay range relative to entry examiner not extracted from sources.
4. **CWA & IFPTE Salary Tables:** Official 2023–2027 salary schedules published as PDFs; content unreadable via web tools. Manual download and review required.

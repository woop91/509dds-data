# Pennsylvania DDS Examiner Salary Research

**Last Updated:** 2026-06-02 (AUTHORITATIVE — retrieved from official PA Commonwealth Careers job specs)

## Administering Agency
- **Department:** Pennsylvania Department of Labor & Industry (DLI), Bureau of Disability Determination
- **Jurisdiction:** Social Security Disability Determination for Pennsylvania

## Examiner Class Titles (VERIFIED — official PA job codes)
PA's actual examiner series is the **Disability Claims Adjudicator** series (not "Disability Examiner" as earlier guessed). Verified against the official PA Commonwealth Careers job specification + expanded pay information ("PayExp") pages:

| Class Title | Job Code | Pay Scale Group | Pay Scale Type | Bargaining Unit |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Disability Claims Adjudicator Trainee | 45070 | 06 | ST | F4 |
| Disability Claims Adjudicator | 45080 | 07 | ST | F4 |
| Disability Claims Adjudicator Supervisor | 45140 | 08 | ST | F5 |

(Related series titles also exist: Disability Adjudication Hearings Officer 45090, Disability Program Manager 45170, Disability Fraud Officer 45100 — not part of the core examiner/supervisor mapping for this card.)

PA uses the **Standard Pay Schedule (Pay Scale Type ST)**; each job code is assigned a Pay Scale Group, and the official PayExp page reports the Minimum/Maximum Annual salary for that group (at both the 75-hour and 80-hour biweekly bases).

## Scheduled Rates (VERIFIED, ST schedule effective 2025-07-01)
75-hour basis is the standard 37.5 hr/week work week for these F4/F5 bargaining-unit classes; 80-hour figures also recorded.

| Class | Pay Group | Min Annual (75hr) | Max Annual (75hr) | Min (80hr) | Max (80hr) |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Disability Claims Adjudicator Trainee (45070) | ST06 | $51,971 | $79,062 | $55,436 | $84,332 |
| Disability Claims Adjudicator (45080) | ST07 | $59,345 | $90,211 | $63,301 | $96,225 |
| Disability Claims Adjudicator Supervisor (45140) | ST08 | $67,736 | $102,905 | $72,252 | $109,766 |

Both the grade assignment (job code → pay scale group) and the dollar ranges come from the same official PA source, satisfying both conditions for an Authoritative tier.

## Bargaining Unit & Union
- **Union:** SEIU Local 668 (Pennsylvania Social Services Union)
- Adjudicators (Trainee + journey) are in bargaining unit **F4**; the Supervisor is in **F5**.
- SEIU 668 covers PA social-service classified employees; the specific CBA wage-grid appendix was not separately retrieved (not needed — the official ST schedule + grade mapping are authoritative). `cba_terms_verified: false` for that reason.

## Notes on prior estimates
The earlier version of this card carried *estimated* figures (SC-09/SC-10/SC-11 ranges from BLS OEWS + job postings). Those estimates were WRONG on the classification system (PA uses ST pay-scale groups for this series, assigned via job code, not "SC" grades). They have been replaced by the verified official figures above. The garbled markdown extraction of `current_ST.pdf` (PDF→table conversion artifacts) was NOT used for any dollar figure; all dollar figures come from the structured PayExp pages.

## Caveat on effective date
The PayExp pages currently resolve their ST schedule link to `current_ST.pdf` (effective 2025-07-01), and the reported min/max reflect that current schedule. The "Job Specification Effective Date" (07/15/2023) shown on the job-spec page is the date the classification/grade assignment last changed, NOT the salary effective date.

This document consolidates publicly-available evidence on Aja James's career at MA DDS-Det (Disability Determination Services) and the surrounding leadership chain. It addresses Section 1 of the data-gap punch list. Sources are linked inline; primary captures live in this folder.

### Inflection points (the answer to "Director → Asst Comm, or direct jump?")

The career was **NOT** a direct jump from VDE to Asst Commissioner. Four distinct title/class changes:

1. **2010 → 2012**: Promoted within VDE bargaining unit (A/B → D, a top VDE grade)
2. **2015**: Bargaining unit flipped from SEIU Local 509 → Managers (EXE) M99 mid-year, while the title was still "Vocational Disab Examiner (D)" — this is the actual moment she left the union. The title changed at the next snapshot.
3. **2016**: First full year as **Regional Director, Boston** ($84,509 → eventually $101,960 by 2019)
4. **2020**: Lateral title change to **Director of Statewide Support** (same rate as 2019, suggesting expanded scope/role rename)
5. **2021 (March 7)**: Promoted to **Asst Commissioner DDS** ($14K raise from $102K → $116K, second raise to $126K in 2022)

Source: `data/pay-scales/_raw_cthru_aja_james_full.json` (raw CTHRU records) + `data/pay-scales/mgr-incumbent-timeline-dds-det.json` (structured timeline).
